FAQ

Questions people ask before getting started.

If you are unsure whether the service fits your situation, these are the most common things people want to know first.

How long does the revision process usually take?

Most projects are completed within 3-5 business days. If you are working with a tighter deadline, you can still reach out and ask whether rush timing is possible.

What should I provide before the work begins?

Please send your current CV, a short description of your career goals, and any specific achievements or details you want highlighted more clearly.

Do you offer any guarantee?

Yes. Every revision comes with a satisfaction guarantee. If the first pass still needs adjustment, one additional revision is included.

Can I communicate in Chinese or English?

Yes. The service supports both Chinese and English communication, which is especially helpful if your background spans both languages.

What if I am not sure which package fits me?

Send a short message through the contact page and describe your current situation. You can start with the problem first, and the package can be figured out from there.
