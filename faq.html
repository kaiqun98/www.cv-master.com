FAQ

Below are some frequently asked questions about our CV revision service:

1. How long does the CV revision process take?

Typically, the revision process takes 3-5 business days. However, expedited services are available for an additional fee.

2. What information do I need to provide for the CV revision?

We require your current CV, a brief description of your career goals, and any specific details or accomplishments you would like to highlight.

3. Do you offer any guarantees?

Yes, we offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you are not satisfied with the initial revision, we provide one free additional revision to ensure your CV meets your expectations.
