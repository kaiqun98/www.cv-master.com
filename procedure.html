Process

From first message to final version.

The workflow is designed to stay simple, especially for candidates who are already busy applying, interviewing, or balancing deadlines.

1

Initial message

Start by sharing your current CV and a short explanation of your goals, target role, or biggest concerns.

2

Package fit

If needed, the right service level can be clarified after that first message. You do not need everything figured out in advance.

3

Revision work

The CV is reviewed and revised with attention to clarity, emphasis, structure, and professional tone.

4

Final polish

You receive the revised version and, if needed, one additional revision to make sure the final result feels right.
